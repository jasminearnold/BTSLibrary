BANGTAN SONYEONDAN aka BTS is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The band consists of Jin, RM, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. The members, as they are known, are talented musicians and avid readers. The group became famous not only for their musical hits but also through the documentation of their journey from being idol trainees to k-pop performers. The group’s fans are called ARMY. BTS consistently shares their lives to ARMY through live streaming on the Weverse and Vlive App as well as posting videos on Youtube. The group has attributed their huge success to the support of ARMY. Through these livestream and video posts, BTS not only shares the process of making music, but also their everyday lives such as rehearsals and downtime reading books.

The group’s leader, RM is a known avid reader and consistently shares books that he is currently reading to ARMY’s. All of the members have shared books they’ve read from fiction, self-help, philosophy and even graphic novels among many genres.

This site is dedicated to and for ARMY’s who wish to learn more about the knowledge that has influenced BTS by acting as an archive to all the books that will help us understand and love ourself.
